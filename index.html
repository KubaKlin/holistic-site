About us

Our mission is based on the holistic nature of human body

All parts of the body are designed to function integrated and cooperate together. We focus not only on the problem area, but we want to find and treat the cause, not a symptom. Holistic approach to perfect health involves three aspects of life; it’s a mind، it’s a body and a soul. These three components have to be in balance to achieve your optimum health. We wish in our Polish Physiotherapy Center our guests can experience Polish hospitality and quality.
